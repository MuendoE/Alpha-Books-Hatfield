Condition guide

Every book is graded honestly so you know what you are getting. Each listing shows its grade, and anything specific to that copy is noted in its comment.

How books are graded

Excellent

Shows light signs of use but the pages are clean and complete, with no tears, no missing pages, and no writing or highlighting.

Good

Used but well kept. Maybe a name on the inside cover or a few small marks. All pages present and easy to read.

Average

Clear signs of study. Some highlighting or notes through the book, but no missing pages and nothing that gets in the way of reading it.

Acceptable

Heavily used, with more wear or more marking than Average. Still complete and fully usable as a study copy. Any larger flaws are spelled out in the listing.

Books not accepted

To keep the shelf reliable, some books are not taken in for sale at any grade. These include:

  • Missing, loose, or torn pages
  • Damage to the spine or binding that affects use
  • Water, damp, or mould damage
  • A strong smell, including smoke or damp
  • Heavy staining or fire or smoke damage
  • Workbooks already written in or filled out
  • Photocopied, pirated, or print-on-demand copies
  • Uncorrected proofs, promo copies, or book club editions
  • Ex-rental or ex-library copies
  • A barcode or ISBN that does not match the actual book
  • Anything incomplete or missing a companion disc or code that the book relies on

Selling a book that fits the grades above? Send it in here, and note any special defects in the comment so the grade stays honest.
